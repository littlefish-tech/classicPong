Welcome to Pong Game

Game rules:

You can choose to play either with your friend or with computer

Play with your friend

If you choose to play with your friend, Press key A or D to start the game

Player 1 press W to move the paddle up, and press S to move the paddle down

Player 2 press I to move the paddle up, and press K to move the paddle down

Whoever got 7 points first will win

Play with Computer

If you choose to play with Computer, Press key A or D to start the game

Player press W to move the paddle up, and press S to move the paddle down

Whoever got 7 points first will win
